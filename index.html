Raleway

Feel of the future

Raleway is an elegant sans-serif typeface family.
It was originally designed by Matt McInerney as a single thin weight and
later expanded into a 9 weight family by Pablo Impallari and Rodrigo Fuenzalida in 2012.

Raleway is often described as a font with a "feel of the future."
Its characters are drawn in a geometric style but also have a slightly rounded quality,
giving it a unique blend of precision and warmth.

The font is open-source and free to use, making it a popular choice for both print and digital media.
It's often used in headings and other large text,
but its thin weight can also work well for body text in the right context.
